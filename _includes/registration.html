Registration

Important information:

Registration deadline
Until 5th April 2019
Registration fee
  • No registration fee for PhD students and PostDocs, who are presenting a poster or holding a talk.
  • 300 SEK for PhD students and PostDocs chosing to participate only as observers.
  • 800 SEK for senior academic and non-academic participants.
Additional information
  • PhD students will be able to receive 1.5 credits from the University of Gothenburg when presenting a poster or holding a talk.
  • Shortly after the deadline all participants get a confirmation of registration, including a notification if selected for a talk or poster.

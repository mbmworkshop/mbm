Workshop on Modelling in Biology and Medicine

Bringing together young researchers in Sweden working on the border of mathematics, biology and medicine.

Workshop post-poned!
Due to the outbreak of corona virus we are post-poning the workshop. It will most likely be held in November 2020.
We will update this website as soon as we have new information.

The Workshop

We are pleased to invite you to our second Workshop on Modelling in Biology and Medicine (MBM 2020).

We aim to gather all young researchers in Sweden working on modelling of biological systems. Our ambition is to give all participating PhD students and PostDocs the opportunity to present their work through an oral presentation or a poster. Further, we wish to provide an insight on how modelling in biology and medicine is practiced in academia and industry.

Registration

Due to the outbreak of Covid-19, we are as of yet unsure if the workshop can be held in physical form or if a digital format would be more suitable.

We encourage you to register anyways if you are interested in participating. We will then contact you as soon as we have new information.

You can participate at MBM 2020 by

  • giving a talk,
  • presenting a poster,
  • or simply as an observer.

In the first two cases you will have to submit an abstract of max. 2000 characters.

Please note that you can edit your registration until two weeks before the start of the workshop, which allows you to change your participation status.

Costs for participation in the workshop are (including VAT):

  • free for Bachelor and Master students
  • 250 SEK for PhD students and other young researchers (PostDocs, guest teachers right after their PhD)
  • 1250 SEK for senior academics (academics not in the first two categories)
  • 1250 SEK for participants employed in industry

In combination with the workshop we are offering a social dinner on Thursday (14th May) evening. We encourage all participants to sign up for the dinner. We are taking a fee of 375 SEK (including VAT) for participation at the dinner which will include food and at least one beverage.

In case you are required to make a payment you will receive an email concerning payment after successful registration.
